F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I trust the companies and the jobs they provide?

    Yes, all the companies with their jobs are trusted and renowned. However, we recommend you to read the contract and terms before you sign with one.

  • Are the questions relevant and updated for various rounds?

    All the previous year questions are the ones that have been asked by the companies already in different rounds of aptitude, coding, interview and hr rounds. The questions are regularly updated by our team members to equip students with relevant skills & practical exposure.

  • Will my data be safe and unshared?

    Your personal data will be completely safe with us and will remain unshared. Ypu can trust us 100% with your credentials.

  • What will be the process after selection by the companies?

    Different companies have different rounds before hiring a candidate on a respective post. We recommend you to wait for the email by the HR.

  • What factors should I see in a company before applying for a company?

    Do see the category of internship and minimum years of contract. We also recommend you to read the company details, whether it is a startup or a high level renowned company.
